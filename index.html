About Me

The author behind the shadows.

I write stories that live between worlds — where demons are more human than they seem, and humans are far darker than they dare admit. My books blend gothic horror, emotional intimacy, and the uneasy question: who is the real monster here?

Publication By Brandon Jones is the home for my novels, short stories, and poetry. Whether you’re drawn to succubi with broken hearts, haunted families, or lovers on the edge of heaven and hell — you’ll find them here.
